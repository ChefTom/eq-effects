# Result Interpretation

## Overview

Result Interpretation ensures that computed outputs are presented in a meaningful and understandable form.

---

## Purpose

To translate raw computational results into:

- domain-relevant insights
- interpretable values
- actionable understanding

---

## Components

Interpretation may include:

- contextual explanation of results
- alignment with domain expectations
- identification of key outcomes or trends

---

## System Behavior

The system:

- associates results with their context
- ensures units and dimensions are clear
- presents outputs in a structured and interpretable format

---

## Characteristics

- domain-aware
- structured
- aligned with user intent

---

## Role in the System

Result Interpretation bridges:

- computation
- human understanding

---

## Importance

Without interpretation:

- results may be technically correct but difficult to use
- domain experts must manually derive meaning
- errors in understanding may occur

---

## Outcome

Result Interpretation ensures that outputs are:

- meaningful
- aligned with intent
- usable within the user’s domain
