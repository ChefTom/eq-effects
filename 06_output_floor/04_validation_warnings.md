# Validation and Warnings

## Overview

Validation and Warnings provide feedback on the correctness and reliability of the computation.

---

## Purpose

To ensure that:

- results are consistent with defined constraints
- potential issues are identified
- users are informed of limitations

---

## Validation Types

The system performs:

- dimensional consistency checks
- constraint validation
- boundary condition verification
- numerical stability assessment

---

## Warnings

Warnings may include:

- inconsistent inputs
- unstable computation regions
- insufficient precision
- incomplete definitions

---

## System Behavior

The system:

- evaluates results against defined conditions
- identifies anomalies or inconsistencies
- reports findings in a structured format

---

## Characteristics

- explicit
- structured
- actionable

---

## Importance

Validation and warnings are critical for:

- trust in results
- model refinement
- scientific and engineering rigor

---

## Outcome

Users are provided with clear indications of:

- result reliability
- potential issues
- areas requiring further attention
