# Governance Floor

## Overview

The Governance Floor defines the rules, structures, and controls that ensure EQ Effects operates in a consistent, transparent, and reliable manner.

It establishes how:

- computation is tracked
- decisions are recorded
- results are validated
- processes are reproduced

---

## Purpose

To ensure that:

- all computations are traceable
- results are reproducible
- system behavior is consistent
- outputs can be trusted

---

## Role in the System

The Governance Floor spans all layers of EQ Effects:

- Intake (input definition)
- Context (interpretation)
- Form (structuring)
- Execution (computation)
- Output (results)

It provides the framework that binds them together.

---

## Core Components

1. **Identity and Audit**
2. **Precision Governance**
3. **Result Reproducibility**
4. **Non-Proprietary Boundary**

---

## Design Principles

- transparency
- consistency
- traceability
- reproducibility

---

## Characteristics

- structured
- system-wide
- non-intrusive to user workflow
- essential for trust

---

## Outcome

The Governance Floor ensures that EQ Effects operates as a:

- reliable
- auditable
- reproducible

computational system
