# Non-Proprietary Boundary

## Overview

The Non-Proprietary Boundary defines what is disclosed in this repository and what remains outside its scope.

---

## Purpose

To clearly separate:

- architectural definition (public)
- implementation detail (non-public)

---

## Scope of This Repository

This repository defines:

- system architecture
- terminology
- conceptual models
- interaction patterns
- high-level behavior

---

## Excluded Content

This repository does not disclose:

- internal algorithms
- execution optimization strategies
- hardware-specific mappings
- proprietary orchestration logic
- performance tuning methods

---

## Design Principle

The goal is to:

- communicate system design clearly
- enable understanding and collaboration
- protect implementation-specific details

---

## Implications

Readers can:

- understand how the system operates
- evaluate its design and applicability
- consider integration or collaboration

without access to proprietary internals.

---

## Outcome

The Non-Proprietary Boundary ensures that:

- the system is transparent at the architectural level
- implementation remains protected
- collaboration can occur without exposure of sensitive details
