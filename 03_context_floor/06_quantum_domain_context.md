# Quantum Domain Context

## Overview

Quantum Domain Context defines how EQ Effects interprets problems involving quantum systems.

---

## Purpose

To support modeling approaches such as:

- gate-based quantum circuits
- Hamiltonian-based systems
- probabilistic quantum measurement models
- hybrid classical–quantum workflows

---

## Domain Indicators

The system identifies quantum context through:

- conceptual input (e.g., qubits, superposition)
- structured definitions (gates, operators)
- variable types (state vectors, matrices)

---

## Behavior

The system adjusts:

- interpretation of variables
- method selection
- execution mapping

based on the selected quantum model.

---

## Outcome

Quantum problems are interpreted consistently within the broader EQ Effects architecture.
